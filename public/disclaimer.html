Tribunals assess evidence on its merits. A well-documented, timestamped, room-by-room report will generally support your position — but the outcome of any dispute depends on many factors beyond the tool itself.

If you have concerns about specific clauses in your lease, we recommend contacting your state tenancy authority or a tenancy legal service before signing.
