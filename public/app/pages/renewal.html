Moving Out
Your checklist for a smooth exit
⚠️ Give notice in writing first
Before anything else, you must give your agent written notice of your intention to vacate. In Victoria, you must give at least 28 days notice for a periodic tenancy or notice that aligns with the lease end date for a fixed-term tenancy.

1
Give written notice to your agent

Save to Vault
2
Book professional cleaning (if required)
Check your lease for cleaning requirements. Most leases require professional carpet cleaning — keep the receipt.
3
Complete all repairs and touch-ups
Address any damage you are responsible for. Use your move-in report to check what was pre-existing.
View move-in report
4
Complete your Exit Inspection
Do a full room-by-room walkthrough with photos after the property is clean and empty.
5
Return all keys and access devices
Return all keys, garage remotes, security fobs and any other access devices provided at move-in.
6
Await bond return
After exit, the agent has 14 days to lodge a bond claim. If no claim is made, your bond is returned automatically. If claimed, you can dispute it with your state tribunal.
