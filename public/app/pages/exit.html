💡 Tips for a successful exit inspection
· The agent will look at exactly what they documented in the original condition report. Focus your cleaning and repairs on those areas.
· Photographing your cleaning is not required but can be helpful — especially for ovens, carpets and bathrooms.
· If you have had professional carpet cleaning, keep the receipt — agencies cannot charge for cleaning that has been professionally done.
· Fair wear and tear cannot be charged to you. Minor scuffs, small nail holes and faded paint after a long tenancy are expected.

💡 When will you get your bond back?
After handing back the keys, the agency has 14 days to lodge a bond claim with the RTBA. If no claim is made, your bond is automatically returned. If a claim is made, you have the right to dispute it. Your RenterIQ reports are your evidence.
