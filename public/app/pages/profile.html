Terms & Refund Policy
Purchases, credits, and your rights
Refund Policy
Each report purchase is a one-time payment for that specific report. Because reports are generated and delivered instantly, purchases are final.
You always see the exact price before confirming. No recurring charges, no hidden fees.
If you believe a charge was made in error or a technical issue prevented delivery of your report, contact us at support@renteriq.com.au within 7 days. We review every case individually.
Purchase Terms
Reports — Paid per report via secure Stripe checkout. Each tool shows the price before you confirm.
Cloud Sync — A one-time $14.99 purchase that syncs your Vault across devices. Non-refundable once activated.
No subscriptions — RenterIQ does not charge recurring fees. You will never be auto-billed.
Disclaimer
RenterIQ is an organisation and documentation tool — not a legal service. Nothing in this app constitutes legal advice. All summaries, comparisons, and suggestions are for personal reference only.
Tenancy laws differ by state and territory. Always contact your state tenancy authority for legal questions.
