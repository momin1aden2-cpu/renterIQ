What RenterIQ is
RenterIQ is a personal organisation and documentation tool for Australian renters. It helps you find properties, document inspections, understand lease agreements, and protect your bond.
RenterIQ is NOT a legal service. All AI-generated content is for personal reference and understanding only. For legal advice, always contact your state tenancy authority.
⚠️ Important Disclaimer
RenterIQ is an organisation and documentation tool — not a legal service. Nothing in this app constitutes legal advice. All AI-generated summaries, comparisons, and suggestions are for personal reference only.
Tenancy laws differ by state and territory. Outcomes in any dispute depend on your individual circumstances. Always contact your state tenancy authority for legal questions.
RenterIQ is not affiliated with any government body, real estate agency, or tenancy authority. Links to third-party websites are provided for convenience only.
