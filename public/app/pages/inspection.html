Questions to Ask
Tap each one to tick it off — add their answer below
💬
These are the most important questions to ask at an open home. Tap each one — record the agent's answers so you remember later.
📋 Property Basics
How long has it been vacant?
What's included — whitegoods, blinds, curtains, air con?
Are pets allowed? (Any restrictions on breed/size?)
When is the property available from?
Are there any known maintenance issues or repairs pending?
💰 Costs & Utilities
Is water charged separately, or included in rent?
Is NBN connected? What speed tier?
Are there any strata / body corporate fees as a tenant?
How is rubbish / recycling handled?
📄 Lease & Application
What lease lengths are preferred — 6 months or 12 months?
How many applications have been received so far?
Is a rent increase likely at renewal? What's the typical increase?
What documents are needed to apply?
🔑 Practical Living
How many car parking spaces are included?
Is there storage (shed, cage, lockup)?
Who is responsible for lawn/garden maintenance?
How quickly does maintenance get attended to?
