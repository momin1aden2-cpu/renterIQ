HOW IT WORKS

Eight critical moments. Zero mistakes.

Most renters lose money or their bond because they missed a single detail. RenterIQ closes every gap — from your first search to the day you hand back the keys.

1
🔍

Search Rentals

Live listings from Australia's major platforms, all in one place. Filter by suburb, price, bedrooms, and inspection times without bouncing between tabs.

2
📅

Book Inspections

See inspection times, book directly with the agency, and RenterIQ saves the property to your profile automatically. No more tracking everything in your notes app.

3
🏠

Guided Inspection

At the property, RenterIQ walks you through every room. Photograph anything that matters, add notes on the spot, and leave with a complete record every single time.

4
📄

AI Inspection Report

Tap complete and RenterIQ's AI compiles your photos and notes into a professional, timestamped PDF — the kind of documentation that holds up in a dispute.

5
✍️

Application Support

Ready to apply? RenterIQ guides you through the process, helps you organise supporting documents, and lets you sign digitally from your phone — nothing missed, nothing delayed.

6
📑

Lease Review

Upload your tenancy agreement and RenterIQ's AI reads every clause, explains them in plain English, and flags anything unusual before you sign your name to it.

7
🔑

Move-In Condition Audit

Before a single box goes through the door, document the entire property room by room with timestamped photos. Compare directly against the agency's report. This is your insurance policy.

8
🚪

Exit & Bond Recovery

Complete a full exit inspection and RenterIQ compares it against your move-in record in the background. Where the AI spots a potential difference, it asks you — you confirm, dismiss, or add context. Your report, your call.

AI Inspection Reports

  • Room-by-room guided walkthrough

Lease Plain-English Review

Tenancy agreements are written by lawyers, for landlords. Most renters sign them without fully understanding what they've agreed to. RenterIQ's AI reads your lease and translates every clause into language that makes sense — before your signature goes on it.

  • Full clause-by-clause breakdown
  • Flags non-standard or unusual terms
  • Covers all Australian state agreements
  • Stored in Vault for future reference

Bond Back Protection

Bond disputes almost always come down to one thing: who has better documentation. With RenterIQ, that's always you. You review each flag, confirm it or dismiss it, and decide what goes into the final report.

  • Side-by-side move-in vs exit comparison
  • AI surfaces potential differences for your review
  • You confirm, dismiss, or add context to each item
  • Complete evidence trail — built by you, for you
INSPECTION REPORTS

AI Condition Reports. Bulletproof evidence.

Agencies submit detailed condition reports. Most tenants don't. That imbalance is where disputes start. RenterIQ levels the playing field instantly.

1

Open the inspection checklist — RenterIQ walks you through every area of the property — living spaces, kitchen, bedrooms, bathrooms, storage, and outdoor areas. Nothing gets overlooked.

2

Photograph and annotate as you go — Tap to capture anything worth documenting — scuff marks, worn carpet, a cracked tile, a pre-existing stain. Add a note and move on. It takes seconds.

3

Tap Complete — RenterIQ's AI compiles your entire walkthrough into a professional, dated condition report — formatted to the same standard as an agency-issued document.

4

Stored in your Vault, permanently — The report is saved automatically and accessible at any time — six weeks or two years later, if it ever comes to that.
