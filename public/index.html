RENTER RIGHTS

Know your rights.

Free government tenancy authorities in every state and territory. RenterIQ links you directly to all eight.

NSW
New South Wales
NSW Fair Trading — Renting Guide NCAT — Tenancy Disputes Tenants' Union of NSW
VIC
Victoria
Consumer Affairs Victoria VCAT — Tenancy Division Tenants Victoria
QLD
Queensland
Residential Tenancies Authority Bond Refund Process QCAT — Dispute Resolution
WA
Western Australia
Consumer Protection WA Magistrates Court — Tenancies Full WA Renters Guide
SA
South Australia
CBS SA — Renting Guide SACAT — Tenancy Disputes Bond Lodgement & Refund
TAS
Tasmania
CBOS — Renting in Tasmania Tenancy Dispute Resolution Bond Information
ACT
Australian Capital Territory
Access Canberra — Renting ACAT — Tenancy Division Tenants ACT
NT
Northern Territory
NT Consumer Affairs NTCAT — Tenancy Disputes Bond Information NT
💡

All services are free and government-backed. If you're ever unsure of your rights, your state authority is the right first call.
