Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Applies to RenterIQ web app and landing page

Overview

RenterIQ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. We've written it to be readable — not to bury you in legal language.

RenterIQ stores your inspection data, photos, and documents locally on your device using your browser's storage. We do not upload, sell, or share your personal documents with any third party.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

When you use RenterIQ, you may create inspection reports, upload photos, paste lease text, or save property information. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to RenterIQ servers unless you explicitly share or export it.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the RenterIQ website, standard web server logs may record:

  • Your IP address and general location (city/region level)
  • Browser type and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

This information is used only to understand how the site is used and to diagnose technical issues. It is not linked to any personal identity.

Lease & document analysis feature

When you use the lease review, inspection analysis, exit comparison, or cover letter features, the text or file you submit is sent to Google's Gemini API to produce a plain-English summary. We do not store or retain the text after the analysis is returned to you. We recommend removing personal identifying information (such as your full name and address) before submitting documents for analysis.

2. How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect solely to:

  • Deliver the features of the RenterIQ app to you
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests sent to support@renteriq.com.au

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating and improving RenterIQ.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your inspection reports, photos, and documents are stored in your browser's local storage (IndexedDB / localStorage). This data stays on your device. It is not backed up to RenterIQ servers and is not accessible to us.

This means:

  • Clearing your browser data will delete your RenterIQ data
  • Your data does not sync between devices unless you manually export and import it
  • Uninstalling the app from your home screen does not necessarily delete the data
We recommend regularly exporting and saving copies of your important reports, particularly your entry condition report, to a secure location such as your email or cloud storage.

4. Third-Party Services

RenterIQ uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load typography. Google may log font requests. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API — for plain-English document analysis (lease review, inspection summaries, exit comparisons, cover letter drafts). Submitted text is not retained by RenterIQ after the analysis is returned. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication, optional cloud document storage and cross-device sync. Stored under your authenticated account only. Firebase infrastructure may host data in Australian, United States or European Union regions. By using optional cloud sync you consent to this cross-border storage.

We do not use advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or analytics platforms that profile individual users.

5. Your Rights Under the Australian Privacy Principles

RenterIQ handles personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). Under the APPs you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you (APP 12).
  • Request correction of information that is inaccurate (APP 13).
  • Request deletion of information we no longer need to hold.
  • Be informed if your information is materially affected by a data breach.

Because most of your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser storage or signing out and removing the app. For information held by us (support emails, cloud-synced vault items), email support@renteriq.com.au and we will respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with how we have handled a privacy concern, you can complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

6. Data Breach Notification

RenterIQ complies with the Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme under Part IIIC of the Privacy Act 1988. If an eligible data breach occurs that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected users and the OAIC as soon as practicable and in any case within 30 days of becoming aware of the breach.

7. Data Retention

Vault items and tenancy records you save in RenterIQ are kept for as long as your account is active. Support correspondence is retained for up to 12 months unless you request earlier deletion. When you delete your account, personal data is removed from our active systems within 30 days; backups are purged within 90 days.

8. Children

RenterIQ is intended for use by adults (18 years and over) entering into or managing residential tenancy agreements. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be noted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of RenterIQ after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at:

support@renteriq.com.au
